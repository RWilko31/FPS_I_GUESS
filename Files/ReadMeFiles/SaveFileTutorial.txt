savefile format
----------------
each section of save data is seperated with "*sectionName*" "*end*" in 
the next row to signify the end of the section.
No caps lock is used in the file to prevent errors.
----------------
order of data:
level info (levelname, checkpoint, playerPosition)
Health (player health, shield)
Weapons (Weaponname, ammo, reserveammo)
inventory()
